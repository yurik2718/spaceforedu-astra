# SEO & Traffic — Business Owner Checklist
**Business:** Space for Edu (spaceforedu.com)
**Goal:** Get found on Google, appear in AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude), and convert visitors into WhatsApp leads — for free

> No technical skills required. Everything here can be done from a phone or browser.
> Share this document with your team if needed.

---

## Why this matters

When someone types "homologar título en España" or "degree homologation Spain" into Google, ChatGPT, or Perplexity — you want Space for Edu to appear. Right now the website is technically optimised. But Google also needs to see that **real people trust your business**. That trust comes from reviews, mentions, and your presence on external platforms. That is your job.

---

## Priority 1 — Google Business Profile
**Time required: 1–2 hours | Week 1**

This is the single most important thing you can do. Google uses this profile to recommend businesses in search results and in Google AI answers.

### Steps
1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and sign in with a Google account
2. Click **"Add your business"**
3. Business name: **Space for Edu**
4. Category: **Educational consultant** (primary) + **Translation service** (secondary)
5. Service area: select **Spain** + any specific regions (Madrid, Barcelona, etc.)
6. Phone number: your WhatsApp number
7. Website: `https://spaceforedu.com`
8. Business description (copy and paste this):

> Space for Edu is an education consultancy based in Spain helping international students navigate the Spanish education system. We specialise in official degree homologation before the Spanish Ministry of Education, university admission support, and Spanish language courses. 15+ years of experience, 1700+ successful cases, 98% approval rate. Service available in Spanish, English, and Russian.

9. Verify your business — Google will call your phone or send a postcard
10. Add photos: your office, your team, your logo, certificate examples (blurred if needed)

### After verification — most important step
Ask your 10–15 most satisfied past clients to leave a Google review.

Send this WhatsApp message (personalise the name):
> "Hi [Name]! We're so glad your case worked out. If you have a moment, a short Google review would mean a lot to us and helps others find us. Here's the direct link: [link from your Google Business Profile]. Thank you!"

**Target: 50 reviews in the first 3 months. Reply to every single one.**

## Priority 2 — Trustpilot
**Time required: 30 minutes | Week 1–2**

Trustpilot reviews are cited directly by ChatGPT and Perplexity when users ask for recommendations.

### Steps
1. Go to [business.trustpilot.com](https://business.trustpilot.com)
2. Click **"Get started free"**
3. Enter your domain: `spaceforedu.com`
4. Business category: **Education → Educational Consultant**
5. Complete registration with your business email
6. Share your Trustpilot page link with clients (same strategy as Google)

**Reply to every review — including negative ones.** A professional response to a negative review builds more trust than ignoring it.

---

## Priority 3 — LinkedIn Company Page
**Time required: 1 hour | Week 2**

LinkedIn pages rank in Google and are trusted by AI systems as a credibility signal.

### Steps
1. Go to [linkedin.com/company/setup/new](https://www.linkedin.com/company/setup/new)
2. Company name: **Space for Edu**
3. Website: `https://spaceforedu.com`
4. Industry: **Education Management**
5. Company size: your team size
6. Upload logo and a cover photo (your office or university campus)
7. Write a company description — use the same text as Google Business Profile above

### Post 1–2 times per week
You do not need to write long articles. Short posts work well:

**Post ideas:**
- "Client story: an engineer from Ukraine homologated their degree in 8 months. Here's how." (no names needed)
- "3 mistakes people make when applying for degree homologation in Spain"
- "The Ministry of Education just updated the requirements for [type of degree]. Here's what changed."
- "Answered the most common question we get: how long does homologation take?"

Posts with a real story or useful information get shared — and shares create free backlinks.

## Priority 4 — Review Collection System
**Time required: 15 minutes to set up | Ongoing**

Create a simple habit: at the end of every successful case, send the review request.

### Prepare two links in advance
1. Your **Google Business Profile review link** — find it in your Google Business dashboard under "Get more reviews"
2. Your **Trustpilot review link** — found in your Trustpilot business account

### WhatsApp message template (Spanish)
> "¡[Nombre], enhorabuena por el resultado! Ha sido un placer acompañarte. Si tienes un momento, dejarnos una reseña en Google nos ayudaría mucho: [link]. ¡Muchas gracias!"

### WhatsApp message template (Russian)
> "[Имя], поздравляем с результатом! Рады были помочь. Если не сложно — короткий отзыв на Google очень важен для нас: [ссылка]. Большое спасибо!"

### WhatsApp message template (English)
> "Hi [Name], congratulations on your result! It was a pleasure working with you. If you have a moment, a quick Google review would help us a lot: [link]. Thank you so much!"

## Priority 5 — Be Present in Expat Communities
**Time required: 30 minutes per week | Ongoing**

When people mention Space for Edu on forums and communities, Google and AI systems treat it as a trust signal. The strategy: be helpful, not promotional.

### Where to be active

| Platform | Community | Language |
|---|---|---|
| Reddit | r/SpainExpats, r/spain, r/learnspanish | English |
| Quora | Search "degree homologation spain", "study in spain" | English |
| Telegram | Groups: "Жизнь в Испании", "Русские в Испании", "Украинцы в Испании" | Russian/Ukrainian |
| Facebook | Groups: "Expats in Spain", "Vivir en España", "Русские в Испании" | Mixed |
| Expatica.com | Forums section | English/Spanish |

### How to participate (important — no spam)
- Search for questions about homologation, university admission, or studying in Spain
- Write a genuinely helpful answer (2–4 sentences)
- Only mention Space for Edu if it's natural and relevant
- Do this 2–3 times per week consistently

**One helpful post per week for 3 months = more impact than a paid ad campaign.**

## Priority 7 — Blog Articles
**Time required: 2–3 hours per article | Monthly**

This is the most powerful long-term traffic driver. Each article answers a question your clients are searching for and brings visitors to the site for years.

**Your developer will set up the blog section.** Your job is to write (or commission) the articles.

### Article format that works
- **Title:** exactly the question people search for (e.g., "How long does degree homologation take in Spain?")
- **Length:** 800–1500 words
- **Structure:** answer the question in the first paragraph, then explain in detail
- **End with:** a call to action ("Book a free document review")

### First 6 articles to write (in order of priority)

1. **"Degree Homologation in Spain 2025: Complete Step-by-Step Guide"** (English)
   *Answers: how to homologate a degree in Spain, what documents are needed, how long it takes*

2. **"Омологация диплома в Испании 2025: полный гайд"** (Russian)
   *Same content in Russian — targets the large Russian-speaking audience*

3. **"Homologación de títulos extranjeros en España: guía completa 2025"** (Spanish)
   *Targets Spanish-speaking clients from Latin America*

4. **"How Much Does Degree Homologation Cost in Spain?"** (English)
   *Answers the price question directly — high search volume*

5. **"How to Get Into a Spanish University as an International Student"** (English)

6. **"Сколько стоит омологация диплома в Испании?"** (Russian)

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Do not buy fake reviews — Google detects them and penalises the profile
- ❌ Do not post the same promotional text in communities — it gets deleted and damages reputation
- ❌ Do not create profiles and abandon them — an inactive profile with 2 reviews is worse than none
- ❌ Do not ignore negative reviews — always respond professionally within 24 hours

## How to measure success

After 3 months, ask your developer to check:
- How many visitors come to the site per month (Google Analytics)
- Which pages get the most traffic (Google Search Console)
- Which keywords you rank for
- How many WhatsApp leads came from organic search

These numbers tell you what is working and where to double down.
